Our Gallary

Igatpuri

This small and offbeat hill station in Maharashtra’s Nashik district will entice you with its scenic beauty and serene ambiance. Igatpuri has many trekking trails, forts and temples in addition to the lush green hills and gushing waterfalls. The Vipassana International Academy, a renowned meditation centre and wellness retreat, is especially well-known in the hill station.

Khandala

Tourists are drawn to this charming hill station near Lonavala because of its clean surroundings and natural bounty. Located on the Western Ghats, Khandala is dotted with verdant green valleys, milky-white waterfalls, and thrilling trekking and hiking points.

Last updated 3 mins ago

Lonavala

Lonavala, also known as the “Jewel of Sahyadri”, is among the most famous hill stations in Maharashtra. The area is rich in natural beauty, with green forests, waterfalls, lakes, caves, forts, and trekking and hiking trails. So, whether you are a nature lover, adventure seeker or history buff, a trip to Lonavala will be a memorable experience.
